My Angel, My Angelina

I don't know if words are enough to reach your heart right now. Maybe they are small compared to the hurt I caused. But they are the only pieces of my shattered heart that I can place in front of you.

These past months without you have felt like walking through a world without light. I keep remembering the small, beautiful moments we shared — the laughter, the calm talks, the way your presence alone could make everything feel safe. Those memories live in me every single day.

And with every memory comes the same realization:
I made the girl I love cry.

That truth breaks me more than anything. I carry the regret in my heart constantly. If I could go back and take away every tear you ever shed because of me, I would do it without a second thought.

You were never just someone I loved.
You were the person who changed me.

You shaped me in ways I didn't even understand at the time. You brought patience into my life, kindness into my heart, and most importantly… you brought me closer to God. When I was with you, faith felt natural. Prayer felt peaceful. Life felt guided.

But without you, I feel like something sacred is missing.

I keep thinking about the verse:

"The Lord is my shepherd… He restores my soul."
(Psalm 23:1–3)

Right now, my soul feels lost. Like the lost lamb that wandered too far. And somehow, the path that led me closer to God always had you walking beside me.

Another verse keeps echoing in my mind:

"Love is patient, love is kind… it keeps no record of wrongs."
(1 Corinthians 13:4–5)

I am not asking you to forget the pain. I know that healing takes time. But if there is even a small space in your heart for forgiveness, I promise you I will protect it with everything I am.

Because my love for you is real.
Not temporary.
Not convenient.
Real.

You are my blessing.
My angel.
My Love.
My Angelina.

I know I cannot erase the past, but I can promise the future I am willing to build — one where I listen better, love deeper, and never take your heart for granted again.

There is something else I want you to know. I'm going to get a new tattoo. Not just any tattoo, something that represents you, the woman who changed my life. Something that reminds me every day of the love that shaped me and the promise I never want to break again.

Because loving you was never something I wanted to walk away from.
And forgetting you is something I will never do.

I would do anything for you… anything except leaving you or erasing the love we built.

All the things I have said so far are from my heart. It might be selfish as you always say. But the love is never been selfish, I want to give you the happy time, the true love you actually deserve. I know you'd say that you're fine now. I know how much my girl worries about life, scared about trusting people just because the most trusted person betrayed her. I want to fix my love, our love back. Even it is dead, I pray to God for a second life as the god himself got the second one to fix the world.

If you ever find it in your heart to take my hand again, I promise I will walk differently this time with gratitude, patience, and faith.

If I am the lost lamb right now, then you were always the one who helped guide me back toward God.

Please… bless my life again with your presence.

Not because I deserve it.
But because loving you is the most genuine thing my heart has ever known.

Forever hoping to say "Hey Iriney, I am love you...." back like I did,
and forever loving you.

I love you ❤️
