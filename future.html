The future I still pray for.

I know life doesn't always go the way we planned.

But every time I imagine the future,
it still looks something like this.

A home filled with laughter.
Little footsteps running through the house.
Quiet evenings where we thank God
for everything we survived together.

This isn't just a dream.

It's the life I still hope to build with you.

"Love bears all things, believes all things, hopes all things, endures all things.
Love never fails."
— 1 Corinthians 13:7-8

And my love for you will always hope, always believe, and always endure.

not The End,
but
A New Beginning...
